His diet consists of thawed medium rats. Our Clown Ball Python's name is Bowser. His birthday is on August 28th, 2019, and he is almost a year old! His diet consists of frozen thawed weaned rats. As you can see, we have a little Mario-theme going for our snakes, and we plan to purchase more snakes in the future!
