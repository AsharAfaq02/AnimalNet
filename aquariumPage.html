Currently, we have a 20 Gallon-Long Freshwater Aquarium with live aquatic plants, and a variety of freshwater fish! This tank currently holds 6 different fish species. We currently hold 20 fish within this freshwater aquarium.



CURRENT TANKS:

20 Gallon-Long Freshwater Aquarium



This 20 Gallon-Long Freshwater Aquarium includes two Clip-On LED lights designed for aquatic plants.
This helps keeps the plants alive as it stimules photosynthesis into the plants, allowing them to absorb
nitrites and nitrates released from ammonia cycling within the tank itself.
The tank houses 20 different fish, including two fry, and 7 different fish species.
This Tank has undergone a few changes in its decor and substrate. Currently, the tank
contains natural hardware-store play sand.
The sand has undergone an extensive 30- minute wash before it was put into the aquarium.
the process included washing the sand in a bucket thoroughly with a hose, until the water
began to flow clear from the top of the bucket.
After placing in the sand, conditioned water was carefully added into the aquarium through
a reverse-syphon pipe. A small amount of black substrate was added between the decor, and under
the aquatic plants as a root support. This tank contains a 150 Watt Aqueon adjustable heater set to a tempurature of 80 degrees F.
Also, we have a 3-step Aqueon 20 Gallon filter, and a small airstone for oxygenation and surface agitation.
