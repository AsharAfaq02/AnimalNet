Ashar Afaq

Hi there! My name is Ashar Afaq, and I am an engineer, and co-developer of AnimalNet! I particularly focus in software developement and electrical engineering. I have a strong passion for STEM, and over time have grown akin to technology. I also have a strong passion for aquariums and animal-keeping. As a child, I have always been fascinated with animals and critters. My favorite place to go was always the aquarium, or a paleological museum full of dinosaur bones and extinct creatures from the past. Now, I own a few Ball Pythons, and a beautiful feshwater aquarium! I plan to extend my boundaries with animals, and even hopefully become a breeder and keeper of a variety of different beautiful animals!
